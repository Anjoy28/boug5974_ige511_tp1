In a small ski resort nestled in the heart of the Alps, Sophie and Max, two skiing enthusiasts, are about to experience a memorable day. The sun is shining brightly, the freshly fallen snow sparkles under its rays, and the pristine slopes seem to beckon them. Equipped with their skis and enthusiasm, they board the cable car, eager to savor every moment of this promising day.

Sophie, an experienced skier, loves to push her limits and explore new slopes. Max, on the other hand, prefers the beauty of the landscapes and the simple pleasure of gliding on the snow. Their friendship, forged by years of mountain outings, is as solid as the peaks that surround them...

But their adventure is just beginning. As they rest, they discover an old map hidden under a floorboard. The map seems to indicate the location of a hidden treasure in the surrounding mountains. Intrigued and excited by this unexpected discovery, Sophie and Max decide to search for the treasure as soon as the storm subsides.
