The storm, with its strong winds and heavy snowfall, transforms the landscape into an icy labyrinth. Despite their experience, Sophie and Max feel disoriented. With only a compass and their instincts to guide them, they decide to seek shelter to protect themselves from the storm and wait for better conditions.

As they trudge forward, each breath forming a cloud of vapor in the icy air, a glimmer of hope appears in the distance: a wooden refuge nestled in the heart of the mountain. The flickering light emanating from its windows seems to beckon them, promising a welcome respite from the elements' fury. With renewed determination, they head towards the refuge, hoping to find warmth and safety.

The refuge, though rustic, provides a safe haven from the storm. Inside, they discover basic provisions left by other travelers: blankets, firewood, and even some canned goods. Sophie and Max quickly light a fire in the fireplace, and soon, a comforting warmth fills the room. They huddle under the blankets, listening to the wind howl outside, grateful to have found this peaceful haven.

But their adventure is just beginning. As they rest, they discover an old map hidden under a floorboard. The map appears to indicate the location of a hidden treasure in the surrounding mountains. Intrigued and excited by this unexpected find, Sophie and Max decide to search for the treasure as soon as the storm subsides.
